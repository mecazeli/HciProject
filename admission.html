APPLICANTS DESCRIPTION
First Year Applicants/New Student A. Grade 12 student who is currently enrolled and is expecting to finish SHS at the end of the school year.
B. SHS Graduate who have never been enrolled in any college/university.
C. ALS Completer
D. Associate, Certificate, Vocational, or Diploma Degree Holder - one who has finished a certificate, vocational, diploma course or associate degree in any college/university
Transferee Applicants who started their college level from other university/school or other CVSU campus
Second Courses One who has already completed and graduated with any Bachelor's degree program
TCP Applicants Applicants who graduated any Bachelor's degree program, interested to pursue 18-units of Education

THE FOLLOWING ARE STRICTLY PROHIBITED AND WILL MEAN NON-EVALUATION OF APPLICATION:

  • Multiple applications in the online admission system. This can be traced by the evaluator.
  • Creation and deliberately using different email addresses to access the online application form.
  • Any deception, falsification, and dishonesty in the declaration of information and submitted requirements.
  • Not declaring the correct STRAND and category.
  • Submission of incomplete, or falsified document requirements.
  • Not following the instructions posted in the announcements.

PROCEDURE FOR SUBMISSION OF APPLICATION WITH REQUIREMENTS:

For Main Campus, applicant will access the link and fill-out the online application https://apps.cvsu.edu.ph/admission/. In filling out the online form, remember the following:

  1. Do not edit (tamper) the information-data of your application form outside the online admission system.
    • The declared strand in the application form and the strand declared in the required document should match.
    • Choose the correct entry type of applicant, do not be confused with the category of applicant.
    • Thoroughly check all information inputted in the online admission system before saving and submitting to avoid typographical errors, wrong spelling, incomplete, or wrong entry of information.
    • Once there is a control number, the information details cannot be edited again.
  2. Applicant will download the accomplished online application form and attach a 1x1 ID picture and affix his/her/their signature.
    • If a soft copy of the ID picture will be used, ensure that it will not affect the format of the application form.
    • Do not forget to affix signature in the application form.
  3. Applicants will select from the admission system their preferred date and time of submission of their application and save the schedule. It is important to save the schedule.
  4. On the saved date, applicant will submit their application together with the complete required documents at the Student Union Building (SUB), second floor, lobby area.
  5. Only applicants who are scheduled on the particular date will be accommodated. No walk-ins for submission.
  6. Once the application has been collected by the Office, applicants will receive their Exam Permit. The schedule (date and time) of admission examination is indicated in the permit.
  7. 1st transferee-applicants will only undergo college evaluation and will not take the admission exam. Result of evaluation from the college shall determine if the applicant is qualified to transfer or not.
  8. TCP applicants will only undergo college evaluation from College of Education (CED) and will not take the admission exam.

THE FOLLOWING ARE THE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS FOR SUBMISSION:

FIRST YEAR APPLICANTS
For Grade 12 Students
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of Grade 11 card;
  • Certificate from the Principal or Adviser indicating that the applicant is currently enrolled as Grade 12 student and the strand is also indicated;
  • The certificate must be originally signed. E-signature is not allowed.
  • Ordinary A4-size folder
For SHS Graduate
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of completed Grade 12 report card;
  • Certificate of non-issuance of Form 137 for College Admission (this certification shall prove that the applicant has never been enrolled in another university/college);
  • Ordinary A4-size folder
For ALS Passers
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of Certificate of Rating (COR) with eligibility to enroll in college;
  • Ordinary A4-size folder
For Associate, Certificate, Vocational, or Diploma Degree Holder
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records (TOR) with graduation date;
  • Ordinary A4-size folder
TRANSFEREE APPLICANTS
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records/Certificate of Grades
SECOND-COURSER APPLICANTS
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission; and
  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records with graduation date
TCP APPLICANTS
  • Copy of accomplished application form for admission;
  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records with indication of graduation date

REMINDERS AND THINGS TO BRING DURING ADMISSION EXAMINATION:

  1. Bring Pencil no.2.
  2. Attach 1x1 ID picture in the examination permit.
  3. Bring ID card, and alcohol/sanitizer.
  4. Keep your examination permit safe and secure. No permit, no examination.
  5. Do not be late. Be present at the examination venue 30 minutes before the time.
