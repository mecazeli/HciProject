2024 PH ROTC Games- Nat'l Championships held at CvSU

In its second year, the national championships of the Philippine Reserve Officers’ Training Corps (ROTC) Games was held at Cavite State University (CvSU) in Indang, Cavite, August 18-24.

Themed “Husay ng ROTC, Husay ng Kabataan,” cadet athletes, coaches, officials, and staff from various higher education institutions (HEIs) across the country gathered for the national championships which aims to promote camaraderie, nationalism, sports development, and the ROTC program.

CvSU tops July 2024 Criminology board exam

Cavite State University (CvSU) is the top performing school in the July 2024 Licensure Examination for Criminologists. CvSU posted a 95.04% passing percentage (115/121) in the recently conducted licensure examination, and was ranked first among the top performing schools with 100-199 examinees.

According to the Professional Regulation Commission, a total of 11,121 out of 22,539 (49.35%) passed the said board exam given by the Board of Criminology in 22 testing centers all over the country.

CvSU produces topnotcher, new registered medical technologists

Congratulations to Cavite State University’s (CvSU) new registered psychometricians!

A total of 182 examinees from CvSU’s Main, Bacoor City, General Trias, Imus, Silang, Tanza, and Trece Martires campuses successfully passed the August 2024 Psychometricians Licensure Examination with a national passing rate of 69.78%.

The said examination was given by the Board of Psychology in the National Capital Region, Baguio, Cebu, and Davao in August 2024.

CvSU faculty members complete leadership dev’t program for HEIs in SEA

Two Cavite State University (CvSU) faculty members successfully completed the 2024 Leadership Development Program for Higher Education Institutions (LDP-HEIs) in Southeast Asia.

Dr. Bettina Joyce P. Ilagan, Dean of the Graduate School and Open Learning College, and Dr. Rhodora S. Crizaldo, Associate Professor V, completed the hybrid training program co-organized by the Southeast Asian Regional Center for Graduate Study and Research in Agriculture (SEARCA) and The HEAD Foundation.

Dr. Ilagan and Dr. Crizaldo attended the face-to-face sessions in Bangkok, Thailand last May 7-9 for the pitching and refinement of their Re-Entry Action Plan. They joined other delegates from 23 participating HEIs in Brunei Darussalam, Cambodia, Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, and Vietnam.

CvSU hosts PH ROTC Games – Luzon and NCR leg

Cavite State University (CvSU) hosted the Luzon and National Capital Region qualifying leg of the 2024 Philippine Reserve Officers Training Corps (ROTC) Games, July 28 – August 3.

Now in its second year, this week-long event themed “Husay ng ROTC, Husay ng Kabataan” aims to promote camaraderie, nationalism, sports development, and the ROTC program. More than a thousand participants including cadets from the Philippine Army, Philippine Navy, and Philippine Air Force from various higher education institutions battled it out in the following events: arnis, athletics, basketball, boxing, chess, e-sports, kickboxing, sepak takraw, swimming, table tennis, taekwondo, target shooting, volleyball, and raiders competition.

Congratulations to newly licensed Master Plumbers!

Cavite State University posted a 66.67% passing percentage (8/12) in the July 2024 Master Plumbers Licensure Examination.

According to the Professional Regulation Commission, a total of 2,936 out of 6,191 passed the said licensure examination given by the Board for Master Plumber in NCR, Baguio, Butuan, Cagayan de Oro, Cebu, Davao, Iloilo, Koronadal, Legazpi, Lucena, Rosales, Tacloban, and Puerto Princesa, Palawan in July 2024.

CvSU is PQA Level III awardee

Cavite State University (CvSU) is a recipient of the Philippine Quality Award (PQA) Recognition for Mastery in Quality Management (Level III). This award is proof that the institution “demonstrated, through its practices and achievements, superior results clearly linked to robust management systems.”

CvSU President Dr. Hernando D. Robles, together with Vice President (VP) for Academic Affairs Dr. Ma. Agnes P. Nuestro and VP for Planning and Development Dr. Almira G. Magcawas, accepted the PQA trophy during the 26th PQA Conferment Ceremony held at the Malacañang Palace in Manila, July 17.

CvSU joins SUC Fair 2024

Cavite State University (CvSU) joined the State Universities and Colleges (SUC) Fair 2024 with the theme “Weaving Culture and Innovations for a Transformative, Resilient, and Globally-recognized Philippine Higher Education,” at the KCC Mall in Zamboanga City, July 9-11.

Amparo, Head of CvSU Extension Services, Community Engagement and External Relations Division (CEERD) and Technology Promotion Division (TPD); Ms. Paula Gie C. Ducusin, CEERD staff; and Mr. Edwin G. Cueno, Extension Services personnel.
